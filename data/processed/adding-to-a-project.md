# Adding to a project

You can add items to your project by using the *+Add* page.

.Prerequisites

- You have created a project.

.Procedure

1. Navigate to the *+Add* page.

1. Select your project from the *Project* menu.

1. Click on an item on the *+Add* page and then follow the workflow.

> **NOTE:** You can also use the search feature in the *+Add* page to find additional items to add to your project. Click *+* under *Add* at the top of the page and type the name of a component in the search field.
